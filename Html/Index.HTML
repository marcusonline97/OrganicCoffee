    Our History

    For many years, John started serving coffee within Italy. In 2013, he went on a long trip around the world to explore the worlds different coffee cultures and he found out about importing beans and selling his own roasted coffee beans to achieve the greatest coffee to offer people.
    In 2023 John sent Unity into bankruptcy and he then decided to become a coffee entreprenour and started CoffeeBrandShop.
